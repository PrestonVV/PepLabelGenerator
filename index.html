Peptide Vial Label Generator

20 × 40mm · fits 3ml peptide vials · Niimbot & compatible label printers

How to use

  1. Enter your brand / lab name — this appears at the top of the label.
  2. Fill in manufacturer, peptide name, size, and date.
  3. Optionally add a dosage and units, and a URL to generate a QR code.
  4. Click Generate Label to confirm, then Download JPEG.
  5. Print at 100% scale on a 20 × 40mm label using Niimbot or any compatible printer.
